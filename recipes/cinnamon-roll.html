The most delicious Cinnamon Rolls

Designed by Freepik

For the absolute fluffiest, softest and not to forget best Cinnamon rolls in the World, follow this recipe from the ambitious kitchen

Ingredients

Dough

  • 180 grams milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons quick rise
  • 50 grams granulated sugar
  • 57 grams salted butter, melted and cooled
  • 1 large egg plus 1 large egg yolk, at room temperature
  • 360 grams bread flour
  • ¾ teaspoon kosher salt
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for greasing the bowl
